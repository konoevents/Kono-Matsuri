Welcome to the Baked Bean Bonanza!

February 17, 2024 | Pongaroa, Tararua District

Event Overview

Hello! I am Milena Kono, from Japan, and I am excited to bring the first-ever *Baked Bean Bonanza* to New Zealand! Join us in Pongaroa for a day filled with fun, laughter, and plenty of beans!

Activities will include the Baked Bean Relay Race, Bean Bobbing, Bean Art Contest, and more. We are expecting a crowd of 22,000, so come ready to enjoy a unique experience!
